========================================================================
  UPDATING SONARIO — DON'T LOSE YOUR GOOGLE DRIVE CREDENTIALS
========================================================================

Google only lets you download credentials.json ONCE. If you replace your
whole Sonario folder with a new version, you could overwrite (delete) it
and have to create a new Google OAuth client from scratch.

SAFE WAY TO UPDATE to a newer Sonario:

  Option A (easiest): copy the new files OVER your existing folder, and
  when Windows asks about duplicates, choose
      "Skip these files"  (or untick credentials.json / token.json).
  Your credentials/ folder is never included in the download, so there's
  nothing in the new version that should replace it.

  Option B (safest): before updating, copy your
      credentials\credentials.json   (and credentials\token.json if present)
  somewhere safe. Update Sonario however you like, then copy those two
  files back into the new credentials\ folder.

The download never contains credentials.json or token.json — only an empty
placeholder — so a clean extract won't touch your files. This note is just
to protect you from a "replace everything" click.
========================================================================
