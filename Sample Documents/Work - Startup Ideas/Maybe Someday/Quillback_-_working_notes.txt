Quillback - working notes
=========================

Author: Avery Quinn   |   Doc #03   |   status: rough draft

The idea: Quillback is a feedback-collection widget aimed at indie SaaS makers.


The problem
-----------
I keep coming back to the belief that AI copilots for small, specific professional niches.
There's a real pull toward tools that reduce busywork for solo operators and freelancers,
and most existing tools ignore it. Note from a walk: kept thinking about local-first
software that keeps user data on the device. People keep running into the same wall: why
onboarding friction quietly kills good products. Reminder to self: the danger of building
features nobody asked for is the thing that actually matters here. When I talk to
potential users, the frustration is never the headline feature - it's the ten minutes of
setup before anyone gets value.

------------

friction quietly kills good products is the wedge. The first user is narrow on purpose: a
